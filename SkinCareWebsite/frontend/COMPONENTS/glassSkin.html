1. What Prevents Your Natural Luminescence?

Before applying structural remedies, isolate the cellular conditions that break down light refraction across the epidermis:

01

Oxidative Cellular Debris Accumulation

When desquamation flags, dead skin cells fuse via surface sebum — scattering light irregularly and casting a dull shadow profile across the skin canvas.

02

Deep Epidermal Moisture Depletion

Without sufficient intercellular moisture, cells deflate — bringing fine tissue textures forward and flattening structural radiance.

03

Micro-Inflammation and Melanin Spikes

UV and environmental oxidants spark low-grade inflammation, signalling melanocytes to overproduce pigment clusters and cause hyperpigmentation.
